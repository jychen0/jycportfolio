Starbound Wanderers

A Runaway Gay Space Fantasy Romance Gone Wrong!

Team: Studio Blue Jay

Role: Programmer, Level Designer

Last Updated: April 22, 2024

Link(s): Work In Progress

Description

Starbound Wanderers is an isometric 2D RPG that follows Zhenyang and Kaveh, two gay lovers who have runaway from their planet to be together. They crash land on a foreign planet and maintain their relationship while surviving the wilderness of their new home.
This game is being developed under Studio Blue Jay.

Contributions

- Designed the map and implemented all art assets
- Programmed how the UI functions for the player, the ability to switch characters and use different special attacks, a minimap, and fog of war
- Learned to work on a full team using PlasticSCM as source control and helped organize task for each people per week on the programming team

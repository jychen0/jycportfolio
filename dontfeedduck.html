Don't Feed The Duck

Ducks sure do love bread!

Description

Don't Feed The Duck is a platformer set in a city with a bakery. Our duck wants to get into the bakery for their favorite snacks. Break into the bakery and escape with your bread!
This game was made for my 'Game Design Fundamentals' class in the 2023 Fall Semester.

Team: Alexander Costley, Jacob Berman, Aiden Morrison, Gage Delgado

Role: Programmer, Level Designer, Gameplay Designer
